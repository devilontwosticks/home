Oxford University and Novo Nordisk have recently established the UK's first truly hybrid academic-industry research institue studying metabolism and type 2 diabetes (see the original announcements here and here).

The Novo Nordisk Research Centre Oxford will consist of eight teams using the latest genomic tools, gene editing, cell models and computational biology at a scale not typically possible within a traditional academic setting; providing a unique research environment for students, postdocs and our 100 full-time employees.  

The interdisciplinary Advanced Genomics team (both wet- and dry-lab) is hiring! I'm looking for highly driven single-cell experimentalists and computationalists who are interested in studying the connection between liver metabolism and type 2 diabetes in a challenging and fast-paced setting.
